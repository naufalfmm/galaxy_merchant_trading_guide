1. The roman digits I, X, and C can be repeated upto thrice(three times).
2. The roman digits V, L, and D cannot be repeated.
3. The roman digits I, X, and C can subtract only digit upto the two biggest.
4. The roman digits V, L, and D cannot substract any digit.
5. Substracting digit only can substract one times, i.e
   IX --> 9
   IIX --> wrong, it have to be VIII
